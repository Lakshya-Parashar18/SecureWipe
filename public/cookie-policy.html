Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings.

2. How We Use Cookies

SecureWipe uses cookies minimally and only for essential functionality. We prioritize your privacy and do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
introSeen Remembers if you've seen the intro video to avoid showing it repeatedly Session only
scrollPosition Maintains scroll position for smooth navigation Session only

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience but are not essential:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
preferences Stores your UI preferences (theme, language) 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or social media cookies. Our website is designed to respect your privacy.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

6. Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies. You can withdraw this consent at any time by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • The website will still function normally
  • You may see the intro video on each visit
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some interactive features may not work as expected

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
